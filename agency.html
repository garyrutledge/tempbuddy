Time & Attendance

The Worker app is central to showing the agency
who is available and who isn’t.

Holidays and Availability

As the worker can inform the agency of days off and
request holidays, this makes way for rostering and
availability capabilities, showing you, the agency, who is
available and who isn’t.

Running late & Absentees

Again the geo-location application creates
pre-emptive agency alerts to inform you if the worker
will be late or absent, this way you can inform the
client beforehand to avoid employer annoyance
or frustration!
